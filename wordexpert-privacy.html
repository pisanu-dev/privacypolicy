Word Expert — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11 July 2026 · Developer: Pisanu · Contact: dev.pisanu@gmail.com

In short: Word Expert has no user accounts and never asks for your name, email, phone number or location. Your settings, favourites and searches stay on your device. The app does send the word you look up to a definition service, sends crash and usage diagnostics to Firebase, and — in the free version only — shows ads that rely on your advertising ID.

1. Information stored on your device

The following is saved in the app's private storage and is never uploaded by us:

  • App settings: interface language, theme, scoring type, selected dictionary, default search type, display and word-length options.
  • Your saved favourite searches and their custom names.
  • The expiry time of a Pro unlock earned by watching a rewarded video, and the Pro purchase state.

Deleting the app, or clearing its data in Android Settings, removes all of it.

2. Information sent to services we use

ServiceWhat is sentWhy
Definition service (hosted on Supabase) The word you tapped and the dictionary language. No identifier that ties the request to you. To return the definition of that word.
Firebase Crashlytics Crash reports: device model, operating system version, app version, a random installation identifier, and the technical state of the app at the moment of the crash. To find and fix crashes.
Firebase Analytics Anonymous, aggregated usage events (for example, that a rewarded video was requested), plus the standard device and app information Google collects. To understand which features are used and how the app performs.
Firebase Remote Config App version and a random installation identifier. To deliver configuration values to the app without an update.
Advertising: AppLovin MAX and Amazon Publisher Services (free version only) Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, device and operating system information, coarse location derived from the IP address, and how you interact with an ad. To select, display and measure the ads that fund the free version.
Google Play Billing The purchase state of the Pro in-app product. To unlock Pro and to restore it on a new device. Payment is handled entirely by Google; the app never sees your card or payment details.

3. Advertising and your choices

  • Buying Pro removes all ads and stops the ad SDKs from being used.
  • If you are in the EEA, the UK or Switzerland, a consent screen from AppLovin's certified consent platform is shown before personalised ads are used, and your choice is respected. You can change it later from the consent screen shown by the ad SDK.
  • California residents may exercise their "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" right through the same consent screen.
  • On any device you can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising ID in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

4. Permissions

The app requests internet access only. It does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos or files.

5. Children

Word Expert is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us and we will delete it.

6. Retention, security and deletion

We do not operate a user database, so there is no personal profile of you to delete. Diagnostic data held by Firebase is retained according to Google's retention settings (crash data up to 90 days, analytics events up to 14 months). Data in transit is protected by HTTPS. To remove everything associated with the app on your side, uninstall it or clear its data.

7. Third-party policies

  • Firebase (Google) Privacy & Security
  • Google Privacy Policy (Play Billing, Advertising ID)
  • AppLovin Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Publisher Services Privacy Notice
  • Supabase Privacy Policy

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the processing of your data, and to withdraw consent for personalised ads. Because we hold no account data, most requests are handled by the third parties listed above, but you can always write to us at dev.pisanu@gmail.com and we will help.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version is published on this page with a new effective date. Significant changes will be announced in the app's release notes.
